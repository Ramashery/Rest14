Our Culinary Journey

Founded in 2005 by Chef Marco Bianchi, Aquarella brings the authentic flavors of Italy to the heart of the city. With over 15 years of culinary excellence, our restaurant has become a destination for those who appreciate true Italian craftsmanship.

Our philosophy is simple: respect for tradition, passion for innovation, and dedication to quality. Each dish tells a story of Italian regions, from the sun-drenched coasts of Sicily to the alpine valleys of Piedmont.

At Aquarella, we believe that dining is an experience that engages all senses. Our elegant interior, curated wine selection, and attentive service create the perfect ambiance for your culinary journey.

Osso Buco

$36

Our signature Osso Buco features tender veal shanks slow-cooked in a rich tomato and vegetable broth until falling off the bone. The dish is finished with a vibrant gremolata of lemon zest, garlic, and parsley, adding a fresh contrast to the deep flavors. Served with a side of creamy saffron risotto, this Milanese classic is a celebration of Northern Italian cuisine.
